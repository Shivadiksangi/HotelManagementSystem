About Us

Located in the heart of the Udaipur,Rajasthan Hotel Paradise offers a relaxing stay with top-notch facilities and services. Nestled amidst the majestic Aravalli hills, Hotel Paradise offers a serene escape in the heart of Udaipur. Just minutes away from the famous Lake Pichola, City Palace, and Jag Mandir, our hotel blends traditional Rajasthani charm with modern luxury. Whether you're visiting for leisure or business, our 4-star amenities and attentive staff ensure a memorable stay.

Why Choose Us?

  • Spacious and air-conditioned rooms with lake and mountain views
  • Multi-cuisine rooftop restaurant and 24/7 room service
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi and travel desk for local tours
  • Banquet hall and conference facilities for events and meetings
  • Convenient location near top Udaipur attractions
